Radarr & Sonarr
----------------
The server keeps the latest Radarr and Sonarr builds in /opt/Radarr and
/opt/Sonarr. Use the `arrinfo` shell alias (already in your profile) to see the
HTTPS URLs proxied through nginx:

    arrinfo

Login with your panel credentials unless you have changed them in the web UI.
